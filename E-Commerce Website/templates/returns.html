Returns & Refunds

30-Day Return Policy

At MiniStore, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

How to Start a Return

  1. Go to Your Orders.
  2. Find the order you want to return and click "Return Items".
  3. Select the reason for return and choose your preferred refund method.
  4. Print your return shipping label and drop off the package at any authorized location.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund. Most refunds are completed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

Note: Some items (like opened beauty products or certain electronics) may not be eligible for return due to hygiene or safety reasons. Please check the product page for specific return details.
